ABOUT THE PRODUCT

Key features of OpenSky-SouthTyrol

Introducing the ultimate web-app for aviation enthusiasts and travelers alike - our latest creation that allows you to track all airplanes flying over South Tyrol in real-time!

With this amazing web-app, you can stay up-to-date with all the flight activity in the region, at any time and from anywhere. No more guessing or relying on outdated flight schedules, you can now see all the planes flying overhead, along with useful information about their flight company, start and destination airports, and more.

Whether you're a curious aviation enthusiast or a frequent traveler, this web-app is the perfect tool to keep you informed and in the know. You can access the service from any device - desktop, tablet, or mobile - and enjoy a seamless and responsive experience that lets you stay connected with the skies above.

Best of all, our web-app is completely free, with no hidden costs or subscription fees. You can start using it right away and get instant access to all the information you need about the airplanes flying over South Tyrol.

So why wait? Head over to the signup now and start tracking those planes!
